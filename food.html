Slovak Plum Jam
Slovak Plum Jam is thick and sugary, and very delicious.

Bryndza
Bryndza is a sheep's cheese which is produced and sold all over Slovakia. Finding good quality bryndza is the United States is quite a chore!

Saurkraut
Slovak saurkraut is often sweetened with a larger touch of sugar after it has been packaged. This gives a interesting and different charachter to all of the savory dishes in which it's used.
